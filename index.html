Video Surveillance and Analytics systems

C-DAC’s Intelligent Video Surveillance and Analytics Solution is a Digital Security Enhancer Application, available for monitoring and operating your surveillance system. With rise in security concerns and easy availability of Closed-Circuit Television (CCTV) cameras, which are meant for monitoring the territory, activities and situations. Development of CCTV systems offers new opportunities not only for recording offenses but, more importantly for preventing them. Our system helps not only to prevent crimes due to visual control but also to identify offenders using the information recorded by video cameras. We use Artificial Intelligence to make systems automated. Our system works on a cloud-based architecture and can record the data on cloud or in local systems or can be used for backup purposes. Our system uses GPU's that makes the solution scalable and faster.

C-DAC’s Video Surveillance systems has following modules:

  • Face detection and recognition.
  • Person distress and theft activities detection from video.
  • Crowd abnormality and vehicle accident identification.
  • Fire and smoke detection.
  • Tracking objects (vehicle, person) in the area of surveillance.

With our video surveillance system customer will be able to:

  • integrate ip cameras
  • set up alarm
  • have full and recorded data about every event
  • record videos
  • control and access software from anywhere from our Cloud-based architecture
  • prevent thefts and damages
  • track people and cars etc..
  • monitor people by using face detection and recognition feature
  • detect fire, smoke
  • detect vehicle accidents
  • apply video summarization and enhancement at your convenience
  • apply multiple analytics module on video or cctv streams as per need
